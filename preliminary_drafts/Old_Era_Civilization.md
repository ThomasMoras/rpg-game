## The Water and Ice Nation

### 1. Geographic Foundation

The Water and Ice Nation inhabits a vast island dominated by a towering snow-covered mountain. This mountain is the heart of the civilization. From its glaciers and melting snow flow clear freshwater sources that nourish the land below. [file:1]

Surrounding the mountain lies fertile territory rich with forests and meadows. The soil is naturally abundant in nutrients, allowing crops to flourish with little strain. Fruits, vegetables, medicinal herbs, and wild plants grow in great diversity. Wildlife is plentiful, and the surrounding ocean provides an additional source of food through fishing. [file:1]

Because of this ecological balance, the nation never suffers famine or drought. [file:1]

The mountain itself is composed only of rock and ice, no mineral deposits are found within it. This absence of metal resources has naturally limited industrial development and heavy weapon production. Their society evolved organically rather than mechanically. [file:1]
